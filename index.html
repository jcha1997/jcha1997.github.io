Welcome! I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Government at Harvard. I am an affiliate of the Institute for Quantitative Social Science and the Center for American Political Studies. I completed my B.A. in Political Science at UC San Diego. Prior to graduate study, I worked at the Pew Research Center.

My current research agenda involves Congressional organization, the development of direct primary elections, and democratic representation in federal elections. I am interested broadly in American political institutions with a particular focus in accountability, legislative politics, and ballot reform.

In my spare time, I enjoy film photography, Laker basketball and running.

I can be reached at my email: jeremiahcha@g.harvard.edu.
