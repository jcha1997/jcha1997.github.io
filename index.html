Curriculum Vitae

Welcome to my website! I am a Ph.D. student in Government at Harvard University. I completed my B.A. in political science at UC San Diego. Prior to graduate study, I worked at the Pew Research Center.

I am interested in democratic representation, Congress and elections. My current research agenda focuses on the development of direct primary elections as well as Congressional committee behavior.

In my spare time, I enjoy film photography, Lakers basketball and Korean food.
